DeltaVision technology ( )
detects dangerous content at source, enabling safer online experiences for all.

Harmful content is hard for platforms to detect at scale but conversely easy for users to discover. In 2022, 45% of children aged 8-17 have seen content they felt was inappropriate or made them worried or upset (Children’s Commissioner Report).

ECT: Early Capture Technology
Our SAAS Technology is built on the principle that good data is a priority for successful AI outcomes. We have spent a decade building a deep understanding of content from dangerous organisations and individuals to inform our Early Capture Technology. We do not use black-box algorithms and believe the path to a safer internet can only be achieved using proportionate, compliant and measurable technology.

What is ECT?
The nature and scale of harmful online content is constantly evolving. Platforms and technology companies across Web2, Web3 and the metaverse face enormous challenges in identifying and eradicating such content in all its varying forms. DeltaVision technology is powered by a stream of privacy-assured data, collated and contextualised from thousands of confirmed sources of harm. It is unique in its ability to capture new content from confirmed sources of harm as it appears, enabling our customers to capture harmful data before it takes hold in the mainstream.
