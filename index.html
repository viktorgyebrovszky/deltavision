Our
Approach

DeltaVision provides solutions to complex challenges facing governments, law enforcement, regulatory bodies and online platforms. Our technology is powered by a stream of privacy-assured data, collated and contextualised from thousands of confirmed sources of harm. It is unique in its ability to capture new content as it appears, enabling our customers to detect harmful content before it takes hold in the mainstream.

Our
Services

Our Customers can Monitor, Track and Block dangerous and illegal content, and use our curated datasets to Train custom AI models.





Monitor
Monitor platform usage by malign actors via dashboards and our reporting.



Track
Track harmful content in real-time with our Early Capture technology



Train

Block

Sarah Ward | Managing Director

Sarah has two decades of experience in online influence analytics, with deep expertise in methodologies that deliver compliant and accurate data outputs. In the past eight years she has delivered global terrorist, extremist and state actor analytics to Defence and Government customers in the UK and US and developed DeltaVision to answer the technical and legal challenges raised by the systematic tracking of harmful and malign actors online.

Christopher Stewart | Business Director

Chris has nearly a decade of experience studying terrorist and extremist networks online, and their use of mainstream and alternative platforms. In previous roles he has designed and delivered CT and CVE programs for UK and US governments as well as for private companies Meta, Google, Microsoft and Twitter. He has also supported Meta and the OSCE in delivering strategic communication training to over 350 civil society organisations across Europe. At Human Digital, Chris has helped to develop and deploy DeltaVision and manages our Customers.

Dr Ali Fisher | Principal Data Scientist

Ali has been studying the Salafi-Jihadi ecosystem for over two decades and in applying his knowledge more broadly, offers unrivalled expertise in developing AI products to detect and analyse terrorist and extremist network behaviours online, including how they have evolved to adapt to Web 3. Dr Fisher's Salafi-Jihadi Blacklight data stream is a core feature in the DeltaVision live environment.
